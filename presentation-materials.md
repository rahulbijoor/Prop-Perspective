## Talking Points and Transitions

### Opening Hook (30 seconds)
"Raise your hand if you've ever felt overwhelmed when looking at properties online. [Pause for response] That's exactly the problem we're solving. Traditional property search tools show you what a house looks like, but they don't help you think through whether it's actually a good decision. Today, I'm going to show you how AI can change that."

### Problem Articulation (1 minute)
"The average home buyer spends over 10 weeks searching for properties, visits 10+ homes, and still worries they're missing something important. Current tools like Zillow and Redfin are essentially digital classified ads - they show you pictures and basic stats, but they don't provide the intelligent analysis you need to make confident decisions."

### Solution Introduction (1 minute)
"What if you could have two expert real estate advisors debate every single property you're considering? One agent looks for all the reasons why it's a great opportunity, while another identifies potential concerns and risks. Then you get comprehensive market insights and a smart recommendation based on the complete analysis."

### Demo Transition (30 seconds)
"Rather than just tell you about it, let me show you exactly how this works. I'm going to select a real property from our Austin database, and you'll watch our AI agents generate a live debate about whether it's a good investment."

### Demo Narration
- **Property Selection**: "I'm choosing this [property type] because it represents a common buyer scenario..."
- **Loading Phase**: "While the AI agents analyze this property, they're considering market data, neighborhood trends, financial factors, and potential risks..."
- **Results Review**: "Notice how the Pro agent found [specific insights] while the Con agent identified [specific concerns]. The market insight provides context that you wouldn't get from the listing alone..."

### Market Opportunity (1 minute)
"This isn't just a cool demo - it's addressing a massive market opportunity. Americans buy 6 million homes every year, and the real estate technology market is worth $87 billion. But most of that value is still in basic listing platforms. We're creating the intelligence layer that sits on top."

### Closing Strong (30 seconds)
"Prop-Perspective transforms property search from a overwhelming filtering exercise into an intelligent advisory experience. We're not just showing you houses - we're helping you make smarter decisions. Thank you, and I'd love to answer any questions."

## Q&A Preparation

### Anticipated Questions and Answers

**Q: "How accurate are the AI-generated debates?"**
A: "Our AI agents are trained on real market data and real estate expertise. While they provide valuable insights and considerations, they're designed to augment human decision-making, not replace it. The debates help you think through factors you might miss, but we always recommend consulting with local real estate professionals for final decisions."

**Q: "What makes this different from existing property analysis tools?"**
A: "Most tools either show basic listing data or provide complex analytics for professionals. We're the first to use conversational AI debates specifically designed for consumer property decisions. The debate format helps you understand both opportunities and risks in an accessible way."

**Q: "How do you plan to scale beyond Austin?"**
A: "Austin is our proof-of-concept market where we can validate the approach with high-quality data. Our architecture is designed to scale - we can add new markets by integrating additional data sources and training our AI on local market conditions. The core debate engine works anywhere."

**Q: "What's your monetization strategy?"**
A: "We're planning a freemium model where basic debates are free but premium features like detailed financial analysis, neighborhood comparisons, and unlimited debates require a subscription. We also see opportunities for partnerships with real estate agents and mortgage lenders."

**Q: "How do you handle data privacy and security?"**
A: "We take privacy seriously. User searches and preferences are encrypted and anonymized. We don't share individual user data with third parties. Our AI analysis is based on public property records and market data, not personal information."

**Q: "What if the AI gives bad advice?"**
A: "The AI provides analysis and considerations, not definitive advice. We include confidence scores and always recommend users do additional research and consult professionals. Our goal is to help users ask better questions and consider more factors, not to make decisions for them."

**Q: "How long does it take to generate a debate?"**
A: "Currently, debates generate in 15-30 seconds depending on property complexity. We're continuously optimizing performance and expect to get that down to under 10 seconds as we scale."

**Q: "Can users customize the analysis for their specific situation?"**
A: "That's a key feature on our roadmap. We plan to add buyer profiles where users can specify their priorities - investment focus, family needs, budget constraints - and the AI will tailor the debate accordingly."
